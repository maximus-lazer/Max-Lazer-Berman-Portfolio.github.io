## Project Overview
Mentor **FIRST Robotics Competition Team 190 Gompei and the HERD** from August 2021 - present. I mostly work on the programming / controls side of mentorship but I have also branched out to doing CAD and Manufacturing. I started as the main controls mentor for the 2022 and 2023 seasons when I was only a freshman and sophmore at WPI. Now I mostly support the controls team as other mentors have came along.

Below is all the robots that I have supported at 190 since joining the team. Here is also the main team repository where you will find all of FRC 190's code.
